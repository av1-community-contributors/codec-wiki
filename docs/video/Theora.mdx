# Theora

Theora is a legacy video codec first released in 2004. It is derived from VP3, part of the same family of codecs that formed [VP8](./VP8.mdx), [VP9](./VP9.mdx) and [AV1](./AV1.mdx).

## Encoding
Theora is frequently contained within a ogg (`.ogg` or `.ogv`) container with [vorbis](../audio/Vorbis.mdx) audio.
